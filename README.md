# 📂 Project: Financial Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **financial sales data** to understand business performance across different markets, products, and customer segments.

Using **Power BI**, I created an interactive dashboard to analyze:

- Sales performance
- Profitability
- Product performance
- Country-level sales
- Time-based trends

---

# 📊 Dataset Description

The dataset contains financial sales data with the following columns:

- Segment
- Country
- Product
- Units Sold
- Gross Sales
- Discounts
- Sales
- COGS
- Profit
- Date
- Month
- Year

The dataset allows analysis of how **sales and profit vary across different products, countries, and customer segments.**

---

# 📈 Dashboard Analysis

The dashboard includes several key analytical sections:

## 1️⃣ Sales Overview
Provides a high-level view of business performance including:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Units Sold
- Gross Sales

This helps quickly understand overall company performance.

---

## 2️⃣ Sales by Country

A visual breakdown of sales by country to identify:

- Top-performing markets
- Regions with growth opportunities

---

## 3️⃣ Product Performance

Analyzes product-level performance including:

- Sales by product
- Profit by product
- Units sold

This helps identify **best-selling and most profitable products.**

---

## 4️⃣ Profit Analysis

Examines profitability across:

- Products
- Customer segments
- Countries

This helps highlight areas where the company generates the **highest profit margins.**

---

## 5️⃣ Time Trend Analysis

Sales trends are analyzed over time to identify:

- Monthly performance
- Seasonal changes
- Growth patterns

---

# 🔍 Key Insights

From the analysis, several insights can be discovered:

- Some countries generate a **significant portion of total revenue**
- Certain products drive **high sales but lower profit margins**
- Discounts have a **strong impact on profitability**
- Sales patterns vary across **months and regions**
